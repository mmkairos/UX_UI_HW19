Hi, I'm Michelle


I am a born creative with art and design flowing through my veins since childhood. Learning to combine both design and creativity has been a whole new world to explore. I am curious by default and am always ready to build upon my skill sets.

I enjoy working with a team to sort out user experience improvements and building a quality product through research and iteration.

SKILLS

USER RESEARCH

I thrive on problem solving and supporting an empathetic approach to execute user goals. Cart sorting, user testing and analytics are some of my favorite methods to find the best solutions.

PROTOTYPING

Design should not only appeal aesthetically to users, it is critical to not only understand users but to also keep your goals aligned with user constraints and the context as a whole. Good design feels with the user.

INFORMATIONAL ARCHITECTURE

Qualitative interviews are a great tool to gain insight into improving findability and solutions allowing the user to "do what they came to do". Observing and listening to testers can improve any design process.

My Work

Kitten Rescue NW SA - Non-Profit Redesign

Kitten Rescue SA NW aims to rescue and nourish kittens in need and provide them with their forever home. We believed a redesign of their app would both draw in the community support they needed and invigorate their cause through improved user experience.

WeMind - Mental Health Community-Based App

Building the WeMind app we believed in offering a safe and supportive community for users. By incorporating features such as mental health books, daily inspirational quotes, and breathing exercises, WeMind empowers individuals to take control of their mental health journey.
